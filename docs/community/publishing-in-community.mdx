# Publishing in community

To share content with your communities, you need to publish your alpha.

## Steps to Publish Your Template

1. Create and Save the template you wish to share.

2. Navigate to Explorer and locate your saved template.

3. Hover over the template and click “Publish”.

4. Provide all necessary details to explain your idea clearly.

5. In the Audience section, select your community.

:::tip
When you click on the input box you will automatically see a list of all the communities that you own.
:::

- Your template is now published!

- All users in your community can view the published content in their Joined Communities and Explorer sections.
