# Managing a community

There are some basic community operations accessible to all users.

![Navigating the Chart Interface](./../../../assets/images/managing-a-comm.avif)

1. **Accessing Owned Communities**:
   All communities you've created and have the admin access for will appear under "Owned" list

2. **Accessing Joined Communities**:
   All communities you’ve joined will appear under your “Joined Communities” list.

3. **View & Navigate to your community profile**:
   Access your profile to view your total audience across all communities, the number of ideas you've published, and your most recent publishes.

4. **Exiting a Community**:
   If you wish to leave a community, simply select it and click on “Leave Community”.
