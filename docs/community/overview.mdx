# Overview

Use the `cm` command to navigate to community.

![Adjusting RPCs on MetaMask - I](./../../assets/images/comm-overview.avif)

The Community feature in Panda Terminal provides a space for you to connect, collaborate, and grow with other users. It’s an opportunity to build your audience, share insights, and gain exclusive information (often referred to as “Alpha”).

By joining or creating communities, you can build an audience of like-minded traders and enhance your overall trading journey.

By participating in communities, you enrich your trading journey through shared knowledge and collective growth. It’s a straightforward process to create or join a community, and managing your involvement is just as easy. Take advantage of this feature to enhance your experience on Panda Terminal.
